What is homeAid?

homeAid is an online platform designed to empower service providers by connecting them with local clients in need of various home and professional services. It allows providers to work flexibly, choosing their schedules, rates, and service areas. homeAid helps service providers grow their business by offering access to a wide range of job categories and opportunities in their region. The platform ensures secure and direct payment processing, letting providers keep 100% of their earnings plus tips. With trusted and vetted professionals, homeAid creates an environment where service providers can be their own boss while receiving support to market and manage their services. It aims to simplify the process of finding customers, scheduling jobs, and growing a sustainable service business with freedom and flexibility. homeAid is committed to helping providers succeed in an easy, transparent, and professional manner.
